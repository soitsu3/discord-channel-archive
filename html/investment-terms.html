A rolling fund that participates in tradfi deals has to meet much stricter obligations than a non rolling fund. Not only that, but the idea of when to price in/out of a rolling fund becomes more difficult as it implies an ability to calculate fund value at any time, which wouldnt be possible if were targeting less liquid, early stage deals. Best way is to either form a legal entity (which will have a trade off for who can join) or avoid doing TradFi deals using the DAO.
