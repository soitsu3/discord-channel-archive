nicklinck 05-Dec-21 08:52 PM
We could set up a treasury with our own token (HYPE) using something like https://juicebox.money/#/ Anytime someone refers a project that gets funding, they get HYPE. HYPE can be redeemed at anytime (for USDC or some stable coin) but the longer they hold, the more likely they will be able to redeem HYPE for more USDC (assuming we do well). The treasury would be a representation of all our investments so the referrer would own a little slice of all funded hyperscale projects. More details: Our treasury would mint HYPE when we make an investment or realize returns from an investment. When we make an investment, we put 200,000 USDC (for instance) into the treasury and mint some amount of HYPE, lets say 2 mil HYPE. The DAO that we are investing in receives that 2 mil HYPE. At any point in time they can "burn" their HYPE in order to receive USDC from the treasury. Anytime we realize returns from our investments. The DAO pays us back in USDC which we put into the treasury, thus minting more HYPE. There are many ways we can distribute the newly minted HYPE to hyperscale community members. HYPE holders can then burn HYPE to redeem USDC from the treasury at any given time. So for the referral program, we can give a referrer HYPE each time they refer a deal that we decide to invest in. The referrer can hold onto this token (which would likely rise in value) and cash out at anytime by burning their HYPE for USDC. To Be Honest: I don't fully understand why tokens like this (HYPE) rise in value as much as they do. Seems largely due to speculation? Some tokens have a bonding curve and a discount rate which effect the USDC per HYPE ratio over time. I mention this so that if anyone understands why why the price of HYPE would rise in this scenario, I'd love a clear explanation. Look at JBX and PEOPLE, they use this same model for a different use case and their coins have become more valuable over time compared to the initial investment.
