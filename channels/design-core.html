PrimalNick 10-Dec-21 08:42 PM
Hey @Core-Design - wanted to share some thoughts and feedback: Phase 2 Product Feedback 1. Simplify: we don’t need a landing page separate from the dao race. Lean into your gut and if this is the direction then focus there. 2. “The Front Page”: Everyone loves competing for something. Right under the “How the race works” add a section called “Top Conentenders” that shows up the top 3 or top 5 projects, so people can jump striaght into some to check them out or so others can push to get on the front page. 3. move over the application section to a “Join The Race” section that has apply with your DAO idea or go to the leaderboard to join a team. 4. if you wanted to increase signups you could require people to login to see the rest of the dao list, apply, or join one of the projects, but really depends if that matters to us Some big things to be looking out for that’ll help inform Phase 3 and 4 are: - what viral loops can we start baking in to the product (i.e. add the short tags to your twitter name to get priviledges in this server, etc)? - what does a successful community look like (are there ways to tangibly track that and add it to the site to guide the leaderboard?)(i.e. community progress, etc)? - how do we make this a continuous game (rather than one winner, we make it a constant competition)(i.e. CoinMarketCap for DAOs)? - how do we better guide people to projects that are actually doing well, not just hyped up (i.e. standardizing and opening the data streams to the community for different metrics like retention, daily active users, etc)? - how do we simplify the experience for the community, making it more enticing to start a project (i.e. Kevs idea for one click to DAO and discord formation, etc)?
