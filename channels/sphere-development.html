WE'RE NOW BUILDING THE DAO SPHERE
The DAO race didn't create an incentive structure where the best DAOs rose to the top. Most applications went unseen & the DAOs who succeeded promoted the race to their communities. This shows great community support but that's just an aspect of a great DAO.
At the core of Hyperscale, we're a fund & a community. A centralized fund is our MVP as we iterate towards a more decentralized model. While we do this our development focus is building the community—that's what the DAO Sphere is.
“The best way that the community can help is by supporting the ecosystem. There will be an explosion of DAOs who will need governance, legal, product, and marketing help. These DAOs will have funding to pay contributors. You’ll now be able to work for DAOs in the Hyperscale ecosystem. DAOs will place bounties for the community to complete in the bounty market. These DAOs can also be thought of as a sandbox for Hyperscale the fund. Learnings here will be implemented.” — What's Next for Hyperscale article (https://hyperscale.medium.com/whats-next-for-hyperscale-1326cfe4f7f5)
The DAO Sphere is the infrastructure that coordinates contributors. The DAO Sphere solves problems experienced by Hyperscale—being transparent, governance, clear direction, & clear community contributions. The DAO Sphere will not only be used by Hyperscale but projects in the Hyperscale ecosystem and beyond!
Here's what DAOs will be able to do: - Format to keep communities updated about internal projects. Helps make being transparent easier. - Actionable ways that the community can contribute. Since a DAO's progress will be transparent it'll be easy to pick up tasks. - Contributors will be compensated through a bounty market. Bounties include building websites, auditing smart contracts, etc. - DAO profiles with a DAO's direction, about, governance form, & how to contribute.

@Core-Dev @Core-Design I didn't do a great job at aligning everyone around the DAO Sphere. The DAO Sphere is not the next iteration of the DAO Race. The DAO Sphere is the infrastructure for DAO <> DAO & contributor <> DAO collaboration. The DAO Sphere displays a DAO's most important info: KPIs. KPIs form two general categories: community & product. Community KPIs:
Grant spending Contributor bounty spending Discord community size Discord community engagement Twitter following Twitter engagement Core contributor spending Distribution of tokens (average wallet size, helps determine the "whale score") How sad would you be if this project disappeared tomorrow (1-10)
Project KPIs:
Protocol revenue NFT royalty revenue Treasury size Weekly P&L P/E ratios
