I hope this is the right place to move some ideas and suggestions that I wrote in general yesterday:
1. A method to register Human and Authentic votes. A simple connect with discord and vote can lead to a lot of false / fake votes as there is no real cost in creating multiple Discord accounts. So voting should be two factor. Ie connect with discord and perhaps email or solve a captcha, enter a otp from email. 2. Concept of fractional down votes. A vote is a upvote. Fractional downvotes such as 1/10th of a down vote or 10 negative votes is a downvote could keep fake upvote abuse in check without downvotes itself being abused. 3. All participants would get their own channels here for Q/A, comments, clarifications, updates etc. 4. Votes alone do not accurately reflect the viability of the projects. What if there would be a 3 tier system for votes: a) Invite a pool of people from VC circles to vote on projects - this will sort of indicate/imply to the community that the projects voted upon by the VC circle voters have possible value. b) Public vote by anyone via Discord and Twitter (via #hashtags such as #hyperSFvote<projectname>) and c) Vote by the actual investors in the hyperscalefund community by signing a vote with their metamask wallet. Each tier could have a some weight or multiplier associated so that algorithms could evaluate the most viable projects to be funded.
